Candon , Ilocos Sur

Candon City is in the lower central portion of the
province of Ilocos Sur. It has a “C” shape with
elevations ranging from 10 to 500 feet
above the sea level.

Galimuyod, Ilocos Sur

Galimuyod is a landlocked municipality in the coastal province of Ilocos Sur.
The municipality has a land area of 34.40 square kilometers or 13.28 square miles
which constitutes 1.33% of Ilocos Sur's total area.

Vigan, Ilocos Sur

Vigan is a coastal component city in the province of
Ilocos Sur. It serves as the provincial capital.
The city has a land area of 25.12 square kilometer
or 9.70 square miles which constitutes 0.97% of Ilocos Sur's total area.

Narvacan, Ilocos Sur

Narvacan is a coastal municipality in the province
of Ilocos Sur. The municipality has a land area of 122.21 square kilometers or 47.19 square miles which
constitutes 4.71% of Ilocos Sur's total area

Sta Maria, Ilocos Sur
